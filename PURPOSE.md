# Shift-Engine: Purpose & Vision

## 🚨 The Core Problem
The restaurant industry operates on razor-thin margins, yet the most critical variable in a restaurant's profitability—**wait staff performance**—is managed almost entirely by gut feeling. 

Legacy POS systems (like Toast, Aloha, and Square) capture massive amounts of data, but they only provide raw data dumps, not **insights**. Managers are too busy running the floor to build complex spreadsheets, leading to four major pain points:
1. **Blind Scheduling:** Prime weekend sections are often given to servers who move fast but have terrible tip percentages (burning tables), costing the restaurant thousands in lost upsell revenue.
2. **Ineffective Coaching:** Managers don't know *what* to coach. They know a server is struggling, but they don't know if it's a table-turn issue, an average-check issue, or a guest connection issue.
3. **Data Silos:** Independent operators or mid-sized groups lack the technical resources to query and analyze their own POS data.
4. **Top-Performer Churn:** Elite servers often leave because their high performance is not objectively measured, recognized, or rewarded with the best shifts.

---

## 🛠️ The Solution (MVP)
**Shift-Engine** is a lightweight, high-impact Server Performance Dashboard. It translates raw, unreadable POS data into an actionable coaching and management tool.

By allowing managers to drop in a weekly CSV from their POS, Shift-Engine instantly calculates a proprietary **Final Score (0-100)** based on weighted metrics:
* **Sales/hr:** Total volume moved.
* **Tips/hr:** Efficiency of earnings.
* **Tip %:** Quality of service and guest satisfaction.
* **Avg Check:** Upselling and menu knowledge.
* **Guests/hr:** Speed and table-turn capability.

**The Output:** Managers instantly see their "Hustlers," "Optimizers," and "Bottlenecks," along with 1-minute, data-backed coaching plans for pre-shift meetings.

---

## 🚀 The Future: A Complete Hospitality HR OS
The MVP solves the immediate pain of weekly performance tracking. The future of Shift-Engine is to evolve into the definitive **Human Resources and Lifecycle Management Platform** specifically built for the hospitality industry. 

Future features will transform Shift-Engine from a scorecard into an operational brain:

### 1. The Employee Lifecycle Tracker
* **Trend Analysis:** Track a server's performance from their first day on the floor to their 3-year anniversary. 
* **Burnout Prediction:** AI-flagged retention alerts (e.g., *"Warning: Chloe's tip percentage and sales/hr have steadily declined over the last 3 weeks. Recommend a 1-on-1 check-in."*)
* **Milestone Tracking:** Automated rewards or wage increases triggered by sustained performance metrics.

### 2. Algorithmic Scheduling (The Holy Grail)
* **Performance-Based Rostering:** Integrate directly with scheduling software (like 7shifts) to automatically assign the highest-scoring servers to the most profitable sections on the busiest nights.
* **Complementary Pairing:** The engine will suggest pairing a high-speed/low-check server with a slow-speed/high-check server to perfectly balance a Friday night floor.

### 3. Automated Onboarding & Training
* **Data-Driven Probation Periods:** Automatically graduate trainees to full servers when their 14-day rolling average hits specific, objective benchmarks (e.g., >18% Tip Average and >$45 Avg Check).
* **Targeted Micro-Training:** If the engine detects a server is consistently in the bottom 10% for Average Check, it automatically assigns them the "Appetizer & Wine Upselling" module in the employee portal.

### 4. Cross-Location Leaderboards
* For restaurant groups, allow GMs to benchmark their staff against the broader company, creating healthy competition and allowing regional managers to spot their best talent company-wide.

---

**Mission Statement:** Shift-Engine exists to turn hospitality data into hospitality excellence, ensuring every server reaches their peak potential and every restaurant maximizes its floor.
